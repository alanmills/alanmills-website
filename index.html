Hi, I am Alan

Hi and welcome to my website where I like to share my thoughts and experience with anyone who would like to listen. As the son of a Computer Scientist, I have been learning about computers and what they can do for a long time. I look forward to sharing with you my continued journey managing teams, designing and developing solutions, writing and speaking about this amazing industry.
