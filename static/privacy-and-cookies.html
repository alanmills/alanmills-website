Privacy & Cookies

Currently alanmills.info does not collect any information about the visitors to the site. This will change over time as I intend to make use of third party services such as:


  • Google Analytics - collect usage statistics for the site,
  • New Relic - monitor the site for technical problems,
  • Mailchimp - provide a mailing list,
  • Disqus - allow you to interact with me and other readers on subjects related to the blog posts.

I am intending to make use of these services to allow me to measure the performance of the site with goals of making the site a better experience for you the reader.


If you have any questions or concerns about privacy or cookie use on this site, please feel free to drop me a line and I will do my best to help.
